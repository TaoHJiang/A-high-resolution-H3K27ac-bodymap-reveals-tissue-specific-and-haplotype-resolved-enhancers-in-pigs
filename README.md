# A high-resolution H3K27ac bodymap reveals tissue-specific and haplotype-resolved enhancer in pigs
Code of analysis during study of "A-high-resolution-H3K27ac-bodymap-reveals-tissue-specific-and-haplotype-resolved-enhancer-in-pigs"

## 1.Introduction

Genome-wide profiling of H3K27ac across multiple tissues from a genetically uniform individual using CUT&Tag. The aim was to construct a comprehensive enhancer atlas spanning all tissues and anatomical sites in pigs, and to identify allele-specific enhancers and their underlying noncoding regulatory variants that may contribute to cis-regulatory divergence and complex trait variation.

## 2.Main content
data analysis of H3K27ac CUT&Tag, Cut&tag aligenment, peak calling, classification of peaks by different assay, consensus peaks construction and phased peaks identification.
